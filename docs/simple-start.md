# Simple Start Guide (For Everyone)

Welcome! If you aren't a programmer and just want to get your YouTube transcripts, this guide is for you.

## What is this tool?
Sometimes YouTube has great information, but you want to read it as a document or save it for later. **Raw Capture YT** lets you grab the text from any video you are currently watching—including videos that don't have a "Transcript" button or are private/members-only.

## How to use it (3 Easy Steps)

### 1. Set up the "Bridge"
1. Open your Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browser.
2. Type `chrome://extensions` in the address bar.
3. Switch on the **Developer mode** button at the top right.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the `extension` folder inside this project.
5. You will now see a small icon in your browser for "Raw Capture".

### 2. Grab the Video Text
1. Go to YouTube and open the video you want.
2. Click the **Raw Capture** icon in your browser (near the address bar).
3. Click the **Capture Transcript** button.
4. This will save a file ending in `.vtt` to your computer.

### 3. Make it Readable
1. Open the folder named `tools` in this project.
2. Right-click the file named **Start-Here.ps1** and choose **Run with PowerShell**.
3. Follow the simple prompts on your screen.
4. Your finished, clean transcript will appear in the **output** folder!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**"Do I need to pay for anything?"**
No. This tool is free and runs entirely on your own computer.

**"Is my data safe?"**
Yes. Unlike websites that ask you to upload a link, this tool stays on your computer. It doesn't send your information anywhere else.

**"What if I get an error?"**
Make sure you have **Python** installed on your computer. You can get it for free at [python.org](https://www.python.org/).
